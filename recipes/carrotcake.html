Carrot Cake

Home

Ingredients

    Sugar Oil Eggs Flour Vanilla Leaveners Cinnamon Salt Carrots Pecans Butter Cream cheese

Steps

    Beat sugar, oil, eggs and vanilla together Mix in flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt; stir in carrots then fold in 1 cup pecans. Pour into the pan Bake about 40 minutes and cool in pan for 10 minutes then turn onto wire rack and cool completely. Beat sugar, cream cheese, butter, and vanilla together until frosting is smooth and creamy. Frost cooled cake and enjoy!
